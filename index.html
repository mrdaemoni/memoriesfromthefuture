This is Memories from My Future Self.
Everything I wish I'd known sooner.

MrHector keeps a vault — hundreds of notes from the books and thinkers that changed how he sees the world. Philosophy, design, stoicism, cognitive science. He's been collecting knowledge his whole adult life. Now he's trying to do the hardest thing: turn that knowledge into wisdom. This podcast is that process, out loud.

The first season is called Learning to See. Seven episodes. One ladder. By the end, you won't look at the world the same way.
